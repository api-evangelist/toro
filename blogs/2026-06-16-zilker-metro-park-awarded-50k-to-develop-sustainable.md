Zilker Metropolitan Park received The Toro Company Urban Park Innovation Award of $50,000 to support development of a new sustainable maintenance facility. The award was presented at the Greater & Greener 2026 international urban parks conference in partnership with City Parks Alliance and recognizes excellence in urban park management and practices.
